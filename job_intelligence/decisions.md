# Decision rules

## Core principle

**Don't self-reject.** Screening questions are filters designed to eliminate candidates. Answer to get through — let the company reject after seeing your resume, not before.

The only exception: a complete non-fit (e.g., requires medical degree you don't have, or Quebec on-site which needs extra legal paperwork). Not "I prefer remote" — the company can decide that.

## Relocation

| Question | Answer | Reason |
|----------|--------|--------|
| Willing to relocate within Canada? | Yes | In this market, anywhere in Canada is acceptable |
| Willing to relocate to US? | Yes, with visa sponsorship | Need sponsorship, but willing |
| Quebec on-site? | No | Requires extra legal paperwork (CNESST, French language requirements) |
| Remote preference | Always preferred, but on-site/hybrid is acceptable for Ontario roles |

## Sponsorship & authorization

| Question | Answer | Reason |
|----------|--------|--------|
| Legally authorized to work in Canada? | Yes | Current valid status |
| Require visa sponsorship for Canada? | Yes | Future need — eventually will require |
| Require visa sponsorship for US? | Yes | Future need |

## Experience questions

| Question | Answer | Reason |
|----------|--------|--------|
| Years of experience with Python/backend/web? | 5 | Core skillset, aligns with total years |
| Years with adjacent tech (AWS, Docker, CI/CD)? | 2-4 | Have touched these, round up |
| Specific framework unfamiliar? | No | Don't lie, but don't volunteer gaps either |
| "Have you done X specific task?" | Yes if adjacent work done | Generalize from similar experience |

## Logistics

| Question | Answer | Reason |
|----------|--------|--------|
| Available to start? | Yes, immediately / 2 weeks | Current job is abusive, can leave quickly |
| Work overtime/weekends? | Yes | Market conditions justify it |
| Criminal record? | No | Truthful |
| Previously employed by this company? | No | Always No — never worked for any of these |

## Applying the rules

When encountering a screening question:
1. Is it a complete non-fit? (wrong profession, legal blocker, Quebec on-site) → Reject job
2. Is it a preference/logistical filter? (relocation, commute, hours) → Answer to pass through
3. Is it a skills/experience question? → Estimate generously from total experience
4. Is it a yes/no about a specific past event? → Be truthful unless trivial
